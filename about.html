Camera-V is a media notary service that attempts to verify and notarize images and video that have been created with the Camera-V app or any app that support the J3M standard.

How is this a Notary?

A notary is...

In order to generate a notarization announcement, someone with our private key submits the document to the blockchain via our Proof of Existence server. The PGP message you see on any notary announcement page is then linked ot a record in the blockchain. You can also verify the notary announcement by downloading the PGP message, and decrypting it using your GPG tool of choice.

We use version control to keep permanent record of these notary announcements. In addition to finding these annoucements here, you can find them on our github page. Every once in awhile, we will commit all new annoucements to a repository where they can be audited publicly.
